About

A natural touch of ayurvedic treatment center in Dubai. Ayurveda medical practitioner with the license of Dubai health authority has been dedicated to holistic ayurvedic wellness treatment center in Dubai since 2010. Hailing from a traditional ayurvedic family in Kerala, she possesses a profound understanding of well-being.

With a bachelor's degree in ayurveda medicine and surgery from Kannur University, Kerala, and certification as a smrithi meditation practitioner from Kase Kottakal, Dr. Anjali is recognized for her ayurvedic expertise rooted in a psychosomatic approach through innovative smriti meditation and counseling.


A fellow in neuro rehabilitation from medvarsity Hyderabad, she is currently pursuing post-graduation in lifestyle medicine from a European university. She is one of the best ayurvedic doctor in Dubai and also UAE Golden visa holder with 14+ years of experience in India & UAE including service at Kottakkal ayurveda centers & Dr. Shyam's ayurveda center (9 years), Dr. Anjali specializes in addressing diverse conditions such as arthritis, neurological disorders, spinal problems, gastric, women's wellness, disorders, migraines, anxiety diabetes, skin diseases, autism, obesity, eye problems & autoimmune diseases.
