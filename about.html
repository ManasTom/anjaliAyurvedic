About Us

Discover The Ultimate Ayurvedic Experience

“Ayurveda for everyone, every day.“

Ayurveda believes that the entire universe is composed of five elements: Vayu, Jala, Aakash, Prithvi, and Teja. And our treatments aim to create a blend between the body, mind and environment. Experience the integral ambiance of an Ayurvedic treatment center in Dubai.

Ayurveda medical practitioner with the license of Dubai health authority has been dedicated to holistic ayurvedic wellness treatment center in Dubai since 2010. Ayurvedic therapies not only lessen symptoms but also reduce the recurrences of illness. Hailing from a traditional ayurvedic family in Kerala, she possesses a profound understanding of well-being.

With a Bachelor's degree in ayurveda medicine and surgery from Kannur University, Kerala, and certification as a smrithi meditation practitioner from Kase Kottakal. Dr. Anjali is recognized for her ayurvedic expertise rooted in a psychosomatic approach through innovative smriti meditation and counselling. Discover the healing power of ancient sagacity and modern science at our Ayurvedic clinic.
