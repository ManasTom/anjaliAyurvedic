Jambeera Pinda Sweda

Jambeera Pinda Sweda, also known as Naranga (Lemon) Kizhi in Malayalam, is a specialized treatment performed by Kerala Vaidyas (Doctors) for treating conditions like cervical spondylosis and frozen joints. It involves a hot bolus fomentation (Pinda Sweda) using a mixture of Jambeera (Lemon) and herbal powders. This warm bolus, heated with or without medicated oils depending on the patientâ€™s needs, is applied to muscles and joints too:

Dhanyamla Dhara

Dhanyamla Dhara is a unique Ayurvedic therapy where warm herbal liquid is poured from a hung vessel over affected body parts. The therapy involves a preparation of cereals (Dhanya) and vinegar (Amla) obtained from citrus fruits, including horse gram, sliced fruits, Navara rice, and millets. These are immersed in water, boiled to extract therapeutic properties, forming a vinegar-like solution. The treatment typically lasts for about 45 to 60 minutes. Benefits:

  • Soothe the nervous system
  • Relieves physical tension
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Revitalizes the body
  • Reduces pain (e.g., rheumatoid arthritis)
  • Balancing Pitta and Vata Doshas

Navarakizhi

Navarakizhi is a unique and deeply beneficial treatment using medicated herbal rice to nourish and strengthen the body. Specially cooked Navara rice blended with milk and herbs then slowly cook. The resulting fragrant mixture is contained in small linen bags or potlis. Navarakizhi is a two-step process. First, specially selected oils are applied all over the body. After this, the rice bags are massaged into the body, with extra focusing on nerve points (Marma).

  • Benefits includes rejuvenation, nourishment and strengthening against stresses and strains
  • Effective against rheumatism, joint and muscle pains, back pains (e.g., sciatica, slipped discs), high blood pressure and high cholesterol levels
  • Strengthens the nervous system, nourishes the tissues and enhances skin appearance
  • Mainly used for neurological disorders, paralysis, chronic rheumatism, arthritis, malnutrition of limbs, and recovery from fractures or stroke

Shirodhara - Oil Pouring On Forehead

Shirodhara is renowned in Ayurveda for its profound relaxation and calming effects on the mind. Warm herb-infused sesame oil is gently poured in a continuous stream over the forehead, specifically targeting the third eye area/forehead. This treatment is known for its beneficial effects such as:

  • Deep relaxation and stress relief
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Therapeutic effects on anxiety and stress
  • Rebalancing the pituitary and pineal glands
  • Ideal for balancing Vata dosha

Netra Tarpana-Eye Treatment

Netra Tarpana is a specialized Ayurvedic treatment for the eyes, involving the placement of dough rings filled with fragrant oils around the eyes, followed by the gentle pouring of sterilized cow's ghee into the eyes. This therapy takes around 30 minutes in total. Benefits include:

  • Relief from tired, aching, and sore eyes
  • Improves eyesight and relieves eye strain
  • Strengthens eye muscles and nerves
  • Balances Vata and Pitta doshas
  • Removes dark circles under the eyes
  • Revitalizes and rejuvenates tired, dry, rough, and injured eyes
  • Prevents formation of cataract
  • Highly recommended for people who spend a lot of time on computers, people suffering from blurred vision, eye infections, or generally tired and aching eyes.

Indication

  • Burning in the eyes
  • Dry eye syndrome
  • Chronic conjunctivitis
  • Pain in the eyes
  • Watering and cloudiness of the eyes
  • Night blindness
