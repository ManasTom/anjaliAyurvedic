Our Services

Abyangam

Abhyanga is an Ayurvedic massage therapy involving the application of warm herbal oils and gentle strokes to the body, promoting relaxation, improving circulation, and balancing the doshas for overall well-being.

Book Now

Jambeera pinda swedom

Jambeera Pinda Sweda is an Ayurvedic treatment where medicated herbal poultices are heated and applied to the body in rhythmic motions, aiding in pain relief, reducing inflammation, and rejuvenating tissues. This therapy is particularly effective in addressing musculoskeletal issues and promoting overall relaxation and well-being.

Book Now

Danyamla dhara

Danyamla Dhara is an Ayurvedic therapy involving the continuous pouring of medicated buttermilk over the forehead in a rhythmic manner, offering relief from headaches, insomnia, stress, and promoting mental clarity and relaxation. This treatment balances the doshas, particularly Pitta, and is known for its soothing and rejuvenating effects on the mind and body.

Book Now

Udhwarthanam

Udhwarthanam is an Ayurvedic treatment where herbal powders are vigorously massaged onto the body, promoting weight loss, improving blood circulation, toning muscles, and exfoliating the skin, leaving it smooth and rejuvenated. This therapy is particularly beneficial for Kapha imbalances, cellulite reduction, and detoxification.

Book Now

Pizhichil

Pizhichil is a luxurious Ayurvedic therapy where warm medicated oil is continuously poured and massaged over the body, providing deep relaxation, nourishing the tissues, relieving muscle tension, and rejuvenating the skin. This treatment is especially effective for balancing Vata dosha, promoting overall well-being, and enhancing vitality.

Book Now

Aavikizhy

Aavikkizhy is an Ayurvedic treatment where warm herbal poultices are repeatedly applied to the body in a rhythmic manner, inducing sweating, detoxification, relieving muscle pain, and promoting relaxation. This therapy aids in balancing the doshas, improving circulation, and revitalizing the body and mind.

Book Now

Njavara kizhy

Njavara Kizhi is an Ayurvedic treatment where warm medicated rice bundles are applied to the body, deeply nourishing the tissues, improving skin texture, strengthening muscles, and promoting overall rejuvenation and vitality. This therapy is renowned for its anti-aging benefits, alleviating joint pain, and balancing the doshas for enhanced well-being.
