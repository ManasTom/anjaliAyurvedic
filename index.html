About Us

Discover The Ultimate Ayurvedic Experience

A natural touch of ayurvedic treatment center in Dubai. Ayurveda medical practitioner with the license of Dubai health authority has been dedicated to holistic ayurvedic wellness treatment center in Dubai since 2010. Hailing from a traditional ayurvedic family in Kerala, she possesses a profound understanding of well-being.

With a Bachelor s degree in ayurveda medicine and surgery from Kannur university, Kerala, and certification as a smrithi meditation practitioner from Kase Kottakal, Dr. Anjali is recognized for her ayurvedic expertise rooted in a psychosomatic approach through innovative smriti meditation and counselling.
