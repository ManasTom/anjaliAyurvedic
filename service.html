Abyangam

Abhyanga is an Ayurvedic massage therapy involving the application of warm herbal oils and gentle strokes to the body, promoting relaxation, improving circulation, and balancing the doshas for overall well-being.

Jambeera pinda swedom

Jambeera Pinda Sweda is an Ayurvedic treatment where medicated herbal poultices are heated and applied to the body in rhythmic motions, aiding in pain relief, reducing inflammation, and rejuvenating tissues. This therapy is particularly effective in addressing musculoskeletal issues and promoting overall relaxation and well-being.

Danyamla dhara

Danyamla Dhara is an Ayurvedic therapy involving the continuous pouring of medicated buttermilk over the forehead in a rhythmic manner, offering relief from headaches, insomnia, and stress, and promoting mental clarity and relaxation. This treatment balances the doshas, particularly the Pitta, and is known for its soothing and rejuvenating effects on the mind and body.

Udhwarthanam

Udhwarthanam is an Ayurvedic treatment where herbal powders are vigorously massaged onto the body, promoting weight loss, improving blood circulation, toning muscles, and exfoliating the skin, leaving it smooth and rejuvenated. This therapy is particularly beneficial for Kapha imbalances, cellulite reduction, and detoxification.

Pizhichil

Pizhichil is a luxurious Ayurvedic therapy where warm medicated oil is continuously poured and massaged over the body, providing deep relaxation, nourishing the tissues, relieving muscle tension, and rejuvenating the skin. This treatment is especially effective for balancing Vata dosha, promoting overall well-being, and enhancing vitality.

Aavikizhy

Aavikkizhy is an Ayurvedic treatment where warm herbal poultices are repeatedly applied to the body in a rhythmic manner, inducing sweating, detoxification, relieving muscle pain, and promoting relaxation. This therapy aids in balancing the doshas, improving circulation, and revitalizing the body and mind.

Njavara kizhy

Njavara Kizhi is an Ayurvedic treatment where warm medicated rice bundles are applied to the body, deeply nourishing the tissues, improving skin texture, strengthening muscles, and promoting overall rejuvenation and vitality. This therapy is renowned for its anti-aging benefits, alleviating joint pain, and balancing the doshas for enhanced well-being.

Ayurvedic consultation

Ayurvedic consultation involves an in-depth assessment of an individual's unique constitution (dosha), lifestyle, and health concerns to prescribe personalized treatments aimed at restoring balance and promoting well-being. Through traditional diagnostic methods and holistic approaches, Ayurvedic consultations offer comprehensive guidance and support for achieving optimal health and vitality.

Lifestyle medicine consultation

Lifestyle medicine consultation integrates evidence-based practices with personalized lifestyle modifications to prevent and manage chronic diseases, focusing on nutrition, physical activity, stress management, sleep, and other lifestyle factors for holistic well-being. This approach empowers individuals to make sustainable lifestyle changes, promoting longevity and enhancing overall health and vitality.

Orthopaedic rehabilitation therapies

Orthopaedic rehabilitation therapies in Ayurveda combine targeted exercises, herbal remedies, and lifestyle modifications to facilitate recovery from musculoskeletal injuries or surgeries, aiming to reduce pain, improve mobility, and restore function naturally. Through personalized treatment plans and holistic approaches, these therapies support the body's innate healing processes, promoting long-term orthopaedic health and well-being.

Neuro rehabilitation therapies

Neurorehabilitation therapies in Ayurveda utilize a combination of herbal medicines, specialized therapies, and lifestyle modifications to support recovery from neurological conditions, improve cognitive function, and enhance quality of life.
