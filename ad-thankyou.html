Unveil Your True Vitality With Ayurveda.

We offer a range of Ayurvedic treatments, Ayurvedic Consultation, including Abhyangam, Jambeera Pinda Swedom,Dhanyamla Dhara,Udhwarthanam, Pizhichil, Aavikizhi, Njavara Kizhy, Shirodhara,Thakra Dhara,and more. Each treatment is personalized to meet your unique health needs.

You can book an appointment at this Ayurvedic Clinic in Dubai by calling our clinic, visiting our website, or using our online booking system. We strive to accommodate your preferred time and treatment needs.

During your first consultation, Dr. Anjali will conduct a thorough health assessment, discuss your medical history, and recommend personalized Ayurvedic treatments based on your specific health concerns.

We offer Customized ayurveda medicine, Panchakarma, Euro rehabilitation, Ortho rehabilitation, Postnatal care, Aesthetic Ayurveda, Skin Disease Management, Menopause, andropause Management, lifestyle medicine, Herbal detox plans, rejuvenation therapies, stress management, preconception detox, smrithi meditation etc.

The duration of treatments can vary depending on the type and purpose. A typical session can last anywhere from 30 minutes to 2 hours. Our practitioners will provide details during your consultation.

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments. Some treatments may also be covered by health insurance, depending on your provider and plan.

It is important to consult with our practitioners and your healthcare provider before combining Ayurvedic herbs with regular medication to avoid any potential interactions.
